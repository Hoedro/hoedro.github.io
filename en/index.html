Services

How we can help you

Six areas of practice, one common denominator: capability installed at the client's premises, measurable results and information that never leaves your control.

Artificial Intelligence and Data Processing

Your organisation's information is worth little while it sits scattered across folders, mailboxes and archives. Through intelligent vectorisation of information and the extraction and combination of data from thousands of documents — reports, contracts, correspondence, databases — we build high-level decision-support systems: complex questions answered in seconds, always with the sources identified.

The systems we install work autonomously: they analyse, cross-reference information, flag what needs attention and prepare decision-ready summaries — tasks that today consume days of qualified work.

Complete independence from third-party chat services: everything runs on your servers, stays operational if the outside connection fails and never exposes a single document to external providers.

Forensic Investigation and Consulting

Our team includes specialists with more than 20 years of experience in forensic investigation and case analysis. We combine traditional investigative methods with artificial-intelligence solutions that multiply analytical capacity: detection of inconsistencies between documents, systematic cross-referencing of documentation and reconstruction of chains of fact.

What sets us apart is absolute confidentiality: the models are installed and run in the office where the case is handled, entirely offline. No data leaves the premises — not to the internet, not even to us.

We also develop bespoke solutions on request, such as conversational assistants built for the analysis of case files and legal documents, at the service of the lawyer or the internal-control officer.

Modernising Culture — Museums and Archives

A digital-transformation service for cultural institutions: creation of intelligent kiosks and natural-language search engines built on the institution's own digitised documentary and photographic collections. A quantum leap in how culture is accessed: researchers interrogate decades of documentation in minutes, curators and staff gain a decision-support tool over their own collections and visitors explore the collection through conversation.

We draw on a broad network of specialists and build every project from the ground up, adapted to the concrete reality of each archive — collection size, state of digitisation, available team.

And because funding is decisive, we support the institution in preparing applications for EU and community funding to make the project viable.

Business Intelligence and Forecasting

Indicator dashboards, market analysis and forecasting models that turn company data into well-founded decisions: where to invest, what to correct, when to move. Clear reports, written for the board — not for technicians.

Due Diligence and Open-Source Intelligence

Systematic collection and analysis of public information on partners, suppliers and counterparties: background verification, detection of risk signals and reports that support contracting and negotiation — with method, discretion and documented sources.

Dedicated Systems and Team Enablement

Development of dedicated applications — light, secure and designed around each client's real workflow — accompanied by training for the teams who will use them. We deliver in working order, with documentation, and stay closely involved after handover.

Philanthropy

Technology that gives back

We make small software utilities freely available, open source under the Apache 2.0 licence — complete tools, with no limited versions and no hidden costs.

Our commitment: these utilities are, and will always be, free of charge. With full transparency: every donor can find out, at any time, exactly which institution their contribution went to — just write to admin@pedrohorta.pt.
